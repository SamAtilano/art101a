Outline for Mexican conchas soup

  1. Take out all of your materials
    • Knife
    • Bowl
    • Blender
    • Cutting board
    • Tomatoes
    • Onions
    • Garlic
    • Cilantro
    • Carrots
    • Oil
    • Chicken stock
    • Soup pot
    • Saucepan
    • Pasta shells
    • Salt
  2. Cute the vegatbles
    • Dice 2 tomatoes into 4 slices
    • Cut onion into quarters
    • Peel the garlic clove
    • Cut the carrot into small bites
  3. Place a cup of water, tomatoes, onions, and garlic into a blender
    • Add chicken stock/chicken bouillon and blend until you have a smooth sauce
    • Set aside
  4. Boil water in a large soup pot
    • Add a teaspoon of salt to the water
  5. Once the water is boiling add in the pasta shells
    • Wait 7 minutes or until pasta shells are soft
  6. Drain most of the water
    • Leave 4 tablespoons of water in the pot with the pasta
  7. In a sauce pan add oil
    • Leave until the oil heats up
  8. Add in the veggies and sauce
    • Add in tomato sauce, carrots, and cilantro sprigs
  9. Pour in 4 cups of chicken stock and bring to a boil over medium heat
  10. Add cooked pasta shells to the pot and season with salt to taste
    • Let simmer for about 8-10 minutes until vegetables are well cooked
  11. Turn off the heat and serve immediately in bowls
